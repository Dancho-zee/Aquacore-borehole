How We Work

A Five-Stage Process, Start To Certified Finish

Every Sediba project follows the same disciplined sequence — because a borehole is only as reliable as the process that produced it.

  1. 01

    Site Assessment

    A hydrogeologist reviews your property's geology, existing water rights, and access before any equipment arrives.

  2. 02

    Geophysical Siting

    We use resistivity surveying to identify the most viable drilling point, maximising your odds of a strong, sustainable yield.

  3. 03

    Drilling & Casing

    Certified drill teams sink and case the borehole to the required depth, sealing out surface contamination as they go.

  4. 04

    Yield & Quality Testing

    A 48-hour constant discharge test and full water quality analysis confirm what your borehole can actually deliver.

  5. 05

    Installation & Handover

    We install and commission your pump system and hand over full compliance documentation, ready for use or transfer.
